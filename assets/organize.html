Israel, 1948-present

"There are only two questions to ask in the

decomplicating procedure: what to hide?

and where to put it?"

-John Maeda

During the 1948 Palestine War, 711,000 out of around

900,000 Palestine Arabs fled or were expelled from the

territories that became the State of Israel.

Since then, 87 percent of deaths have been Palestinian

and only 13 percent Israeli.
